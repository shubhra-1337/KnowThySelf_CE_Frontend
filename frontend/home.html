Our Philosophy

Knowing yourself is the start of all wisdom. This platform helps you observe your natural tendencies — how you process information, interact socially, make decisions, and organize life. The goal is not to label you, but to help you understand your patterns so you can grow with clarity. Our MBTI-based analysis highlights strengths, potential blind spots, and areas where you can improve. With structured insights and recommendations, you can align your habits with your personality and make better choices in academics, careers, relationships, and personal growth.

About Us

Know Thy Self is a student-built project that blends psychology and technology to deliver an interactive personality assessment experience. It includes a structured MBTI quiz, real-time scoring, and an insight dashboard that visually displays your personality dimensions. Additionally, our recommendation module suggests books, songs, movies, and career pathways tailored to your personality type. This project was developed as a practical implementation of full-stack concepts while creating something engaging, informative, and helpful for self-discovery.
